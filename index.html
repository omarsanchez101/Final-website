Artist Statement


The two main reasons why I created my work are very simple. One, is that it’s my passion and two, is that it’s my career. The following work that you will see are some of my best work— that I am very proud of. There is so much fun in making art and I had a blast making each and every single piece. I hope that I can properly convey the feeling of the art work across as I intended. I must admit there is no deeper meaning behind my work but more of appreciation I have for the main focus in the art. I do believe that some of you may not know the subject matter in my art but I would like for you to take this opportunity to open up your mind and see why I appreciate the subject. Who knows you may find an appreciation for it for yourself as well.
